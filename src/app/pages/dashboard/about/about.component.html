Company overview

Applications Emicon delivers IT enabled business transformation initiatives within organizations. These help organizations in the public, and private sectors operate more efficiently and profitably. By combining in-depth understanding of our clients' businesses with vast industry and consulting expertise with Big 4 consulting organizations, we take on our clients' challenges jointly and develop specific specialized solutions to address them. Through our highly collaborative consulting style, we work with our clients to transform their business, enabling them to focus on what they do best and leave their back office and operational enablement on us.

We are not product driven organization but we believe in how to drive the best solution and value proposition. We believe in delivering business value and therefore achieve a better ROI and TCO to our client. Our offerings span across Business and Technology Consulting, Application Services, Systems Integration, Custom Software Development, Business Process re-engineering, Change & Knowledge Management, Mobile Application Development, IT Infrastructure Services and Business Process Management and across Enterprise Content Management (ECM).
